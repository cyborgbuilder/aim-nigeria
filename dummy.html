Event Overview

Water Management CONFERENCE - Dubai, UAE

As Dubai continues its rapid urban expansion and pursues ambitious sustainability goals, efficient water management is a top priority. The Water Management Conference will bring together government authorities, industry leaders, engineers, and technology innovators to address key challenges and explore cutting-edge solutions for stormwater control, wastewater recycling, and flood resilience in the UAE.

This one-day conference will focus on advanced technologies, regulatory frameworks, and sustainable strategies to enhance Dubai’s water infrastructure. With the UAE’s commitment to smart city initiatives and climate resilience, experts will share insights on adapting global best practices to the region and how public-private partnerships (PPPs) can drive innovation, investment, and long-term water sustainability.
